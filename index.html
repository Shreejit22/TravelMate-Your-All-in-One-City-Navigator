Explore the City with TravelMate

Belgaum (Belagavi) offers efficient bus services for both local and long-distance travel. The North Western Karnataka Road Transport Corporation (NWKRTC) operates city buses that connect key areas such as Tilakwadi, Shahapur, Nehru Nagar, and the Central Bus Terminal (CBT) with affordable fares and service timings from 6 AM to 10 PM. For intercity and interstate travel, Belgaum is well-connected to major destinations like Goa, Pune, and Bangalore through buses run by NWKRTC, KSRTC, and private operators like VRL and SRS Travels, offering a range of regular, semi-luxury, and luxury options.
